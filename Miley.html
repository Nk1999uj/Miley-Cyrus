Miley Cyrus is an iconic American singer, songwriter, and actress who rose to fame as the star of Disney Channel's Hannah Montana, where she showcased her talent as both an actress and musician. Over the years, she has evolved into a multifaceted artist known for her powerful vocals, genre-blending music, and bold stage presence. With chart-topping hits like "Wrecking Ball," "Party in the U.S.A.," and "Flowers," Miley has consistently reinvented her sound. Beyond music, she is a passionate advocate for LGBTQ+ rights, mental health awareness, and animal welfare. Her fearless creativity and authenticity continue to inspire fans worldwide
